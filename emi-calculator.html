✅ What is EMI Calculator?


An EMI Calculator helps you calculate your monthly loan payment (EMI), total interest payable, and total amount payable based on loan details.


✅ How to Use EMI Calculator?


> Enter loan amount


> Enter interest rate


> Enter tenure


> Click Calculate EMI
